Text Preprocessing

Prepares the text for NLP tasks like text classification.

ⓘ
Converts noisy text into a lowercase format without punctuation marks, emojis, URLs, or HTML tags.
ⓘ
Stopwords are words that are used so often ("the", "is", "and") that they carry little unique meaning.
ⓘ
Reduces words to their dictionary form, e.g "better" becomes "good" and "is" becomes "be".
ⓘ
Reduces words to their root form (stem), e.g "running", "runs", and "runner" all become "run".

Bag of Words

Splits the text from the selected box into words and counts their occurrences.

Text Classification

Attempts to determine the best fitting category for the text from the selected box.
